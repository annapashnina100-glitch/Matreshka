Ingredients

  • Beef or chicken broth – 1 L
  • Pickles – 2 pcs
  • Barley – 100 g
  • Potatoes – 2 pcs
  • Carrot, onion – 1 each
  • Pickle brine – 100 ml

Instructions

  1. Cook barley in broth until soft.
  2. Add potatoes, carrot, onion, and chopped pickles.
  3. Pour in pickle brine and simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Serve with sour cream and greens.
