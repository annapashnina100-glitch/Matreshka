Chicken Kiev

Home Back to Southern Region

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast – 2 pcs
  • Butter – 50 g
  • Breadcrumbs – 100 g
  • Egg – 1
  • Flour – 50 g
  • Salt, pepper, herbs – to taste

Instructions

  1. Flatten chicken fillets, place butter and herbs inside.
  2. Roll up, coat in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs.
  3. Fry until golden, then bake for 10 minutes.
