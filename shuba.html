Herring Under a Fur Coat

Home Back to Moscow Region

Ingredients

  • Salted herring – 1 fillet
  • Boiled potatoes – 2 pcs
  • Boiled carrots – 2 pcs
  • Boiled beets – 2 pcs
  • Boiled eggs – 2 pcs
  • Mayonnaise – to taste

Instructions

  1. Layer grated potatoes, herring, carrots, eggs, and beets.
  2. Spread mayonnaise between layers.
  3. Refrigerate for a few hours before serving.
