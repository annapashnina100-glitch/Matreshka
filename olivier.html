Ingredients

  • Boiled potatoes – 3 pcs
  • Boiled carrots – 2 pcs
  • Pickles – 2 pcs
  • Boiled eggs – 3 pcs
  • Peas – 1 can
  • Cooked chicken or sausage – 200 g
  • Mayonnaise – 3 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Dice all ingredients finely.
  2. Combine in a bowl and add mayonnaise.
  3. Mix well and chill before serving.
