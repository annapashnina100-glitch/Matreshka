Moscow & Central Russia

Classic recipes commonly found in Moscow, St Petersburg and central regions.

Borscht

Beetroot-based soup with beef, cabbage and sour cream — tangy and rich.

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 500 g beef or vegetable stock
  • 3 beets, 2 potatoes, 1 carrot, 1 onion, cabbage
  • Tomato paste, salt, pepper, dill, sour cream

Steps

  1. Sauté onion/carrot, add grated beets and tomato paste.
  2. Add stock, potatoes and cabbage, simmer 25–30 min.
  3. Serve hot with sour cream and dill.

Chicken Kiev (Kotleta po-Kievsky)

Crispy rolled cutlet with herbed butter inside — juicy and buttery.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts, butter, garlic, parsley, breadcrumbs, eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare herbed butter, chill into logs. Flatten chicken, place butter, roll and bread.
  2. Fry then bake until cooked through.

Fried Potatoes

Crispy pan-fried potatoes — simple comfort food that complements many dishes.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes, onion, salt, pepper, sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes thin, dry, and fry in hot oil until golden and crisp.
  2. Season and serve hot.
