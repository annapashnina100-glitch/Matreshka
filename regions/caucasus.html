Southern Russia & Caucasus

Bright herbs, grilled meat and bold flavors influenced by Caucasian traditions.

Shashlik

Marinated skewered meat, grilled over coals — smoky and fragrant.

Ingredients

  • Pork or lamb, onion, vinegar, oil, pepper, paprika, herbs.

Steps

  1. Marinate meat overnight, skewer and grill over hot coals until charred and juicy.

Herring under a Fur Coat (Shuba)

Layered salad with pickled herring, boiled vegetables and beetroot — colorful and rich.

Ingredients & Steps

The salad layers herring, onion, potatoes, carrots, beets and mayonnaise. Chill thoroughly before serving.
