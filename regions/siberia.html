Siberia

Rugged cuisine for cold climates — dumplings, preserved fish and hearty stews.

Pelmeni

Meat-filled dumplings boiled and served with butter or sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Flour, eggs, water; minced beef/pork; onion; salt; pepper.

Steps

  1. Make dough, roll thin. Fill with meat, pinch into small dumplings.
  2. Boil until they float and serve with butter/sour cream.

Stroganina

Thinly sliced frozen fish served raw — an arctic delicacy.

Notes

Preparation requires properly frozen fresh fish and traditional serving; typically salted or dipped in sauces.
