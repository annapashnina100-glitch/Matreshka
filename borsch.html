Borsch

Ingredients

  • Beets – 2 pcs
  • Potatoes – 3 pcs
  • Cabbage – 200 g
  • Carrot – 1 pc
  • Beef broth – 1 L
  • Tomato paste – 2 tbsp
  • Garlic, salt, pepper – to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil beef broth with potatoes.
  2. Fry beets, carrots, and onions with tomato paste.
  3. Add everything to broth and simmer 15–20 minutes.
  4. Serve with sour cream and dill.
