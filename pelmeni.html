Pelmeni

Home Back to Siberia Region

Ingredients

  • Flour – 400 g
  • Water – 150 ml
  • Egg – 1
  • Minced beef and pork – 400 g
  • Onion – 1
  • Salt, pepper – to taste

Instructions

  1. Knead dough from flour, egg, and water.
  2. Mix minced meat with onion, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form small dumplings and boil until they float.
  4. Serve with butter or sour cream.
