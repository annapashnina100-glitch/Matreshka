Ingredients

  • Flour – 400 g
  • Milk – 150 ml
  • Egg – 1
  • Cheese (suluguni or feta) – 200 g
  • Butter – 50 g

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough from flour, milk, and egg.
  2. Shape into boat form, fill with cheese.
  3. Bake at 200°C until golden, top with butter and egg yolk.
