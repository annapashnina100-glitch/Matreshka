Ingredients

  • Boiled potatoes – 3 pcs
  • Boiled eggs – 3 pcs
  • Cucumber – 2 pcs
  • Sausage or ham – 200 g
  • Kvass or kefir – 1 L
  • Green onions, dill, sour cream – to taste

Instructions

  1. Dice all ingredients.
  2. Mix in a large bowl and pour over kvass or kefir.
  3. Add sour cream before serving.
